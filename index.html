Professional Biography

I am currently pursuing my M.Sc. (Engg.) in Computer Science and Engineering at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), Bangladesh. I hold a B.Sc. in CSE from Port City International University (PCIU). My research journey focuses on the intersection of deep learning and interpretability, particularly through **Explainable AI (XAI)** using LIME and SHAP.

My contributions include work on fine-grained hate speech detection, speech emotion recognition in Bangla, and abstractive summarization for Urdu. I have a strong foundation in **Data Mining**, applying Association Rule Mining to healthcare (stroke risk) and environmental data.
